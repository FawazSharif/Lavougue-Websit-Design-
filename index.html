About Us

Welcome to Lavogue, your number one source for all things Dresses and Fabrics. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of products, with a focus on Beauty, Comfort and Elegance ,Founded in 2021 by Aishat Muhammad & Fadilah Muhammad, Lavogue is starting its journey from its humble beginnings in a simple home in Abuja, Nigeria.
When Aishat & Fadilah first started out, Their passion for fashion designing drove them to start creating these amazing designs you can see on our page, and gave them the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming clothing brand. We now serve customers all over Nigeria, and are thrilled to be a part of the fashion industry.
We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,

Aishat & Fadilah
